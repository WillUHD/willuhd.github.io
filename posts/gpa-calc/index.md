# GPA Calculator

Keep track of your academic progress with our simple and intuitive GPA Calculator. Designed for students who want a clean, no-nonsense way to manage their grades.

### Features
- Support for weighted and unweighted GPA.
- Real-time calculations as you type.
- Data is saved locally on your device.
